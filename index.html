About us

"Welcome to Verbia, the epitome of linguistic excellence and literary fervor at Nam HSS Peringathur! We are thrilled to present to you a celebration of the English language like never before. Verbia is not just an event; it's a platform where words come to life, where creativity knows no bounds, and where the magic of language unfolds. Our English fest is a melting pot of talent, bringing together students who share a passion for literature, eloquence, and the art of expression. From gripping debates and spellbinding oratory to creative writing and dramatic performances, Verbia promises a kaleidoscope of linguistic brilliance. Join us in this literary extravaganza as we embark on a journey through the enchanting realms of words and ideas. Let Verbia be the catalyst for inspiration, camaraderie, and a celebration of the rich tapestry of the English language. Get ready to be captivated, enthralled, and immersed in the world of Verbia!"

What is our goal

The goal of Verbia, our English fest at Nam HSS Peringathur, is to create an inclusive and vibrant platform that celebrates the richness of the English language. We aim to foster a love for literature, language, and creative expression among our students. Through a diverse range of events such as debates, oratory, creative writing, and dramatic performances, we strive to encourage and showcase the unique talents and perspectives of our participants. Verbia is not just about competition; it's about collaboration, inspiration, and the joy of exploring the endless possibilities that language offers. Our ultimate goal is to create an atmosphere where students can engage, learn, and appreciate the beauty of English in all its forms, fostering a lifelong connection with the power of words.
